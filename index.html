Bowling

World-Class Bowling Experience

Welcome to the Plaza Bowl Website! Plaza Bowl has been in business since 1960s. We have worked hard during the summer months to renovate and improve our facilities.

We have installed 24 synthetic pro lanes by CERTIFIED Brunswick Professionals Provides. Full lanes and Approaches are the most popular with Glow-In-The-Dark. The Down Lane Markers add more targets for bowler to improve their game.

We also have installed 6 new lanes of 10-pin bowling and a new scoring system enabling customers to enjoy one of North America's most popular games.

We have added Bowling Music Video. It is free to select your Music and Video. We have D J lights, Speakers and the most popular bowling music video cross North American. Our centre also has the Carpet, full lane and approaches with Glow in the Dark to enhance our Rock Glow bowling experience.

We have 18 five pin lanes, 6 ten pin lanes, and offer league bowling for all ages (Youth, Adult and Senior).

Arcade Games

Exciting Arcade Experience

Step into our vibrant arcade room filled with the latest and classic arcade games. Challenge your friends to a game of skill and see who comes out on top!

Our arcade features state-of-the-art gaming stations with a variety of games for all ages. From racing simulators to shooting galleries, there's something for everyone in the family.

With comfortable seating areas and vibrant lighting, our arcade is the perfect place to unwind and have fun with friends.

King Pin Restaurant

Fully Licensed Restaurant & Banquet Facility

Our King Pin Restaurant offers full banquet facilities that can seat up to 155 people. It can be used for birthday parties, stag and does, Christmas and corporate parties.

Enjoy delicious meals and beverages in a comfortable setting. Our restaurant offers a variety of menu options to satisfy every taste.

Whether you're planning a small gathering or a large celebration, our banquet facilities provide the perfect venue for your special occasion.

Billiards

Premium Billiard Tables

We have 8 full size billiard tables and 3 nine ball tables for you to enjoy. Whether you're a seasoned player or just learning, our tables provide the perfect playing surface.

Challenge friends to a game of 8-ball, 9-ball, or straight pool in our comfortable and well-lit billiards area.

Our tables are maintained to professional standards, ensuring a true roll and consistent play.

AR Darts

Augmented Reality Darts Experience

Experience the future of darts with our cutting-edge AR Darts system. This innovative technology combines traditional darts gameplay with augmented reality for an immersive experience.

Our AR Darts setup features interactive targets, digital scoring, and a variety of game modes that bring a new level of excitement to this classic game.

Perfect for both casual players and competitive enthusiasts looking for a unique twist on a beloved pastime.

Massage Chairs

Relaxation & Recovery

After an intense bowling session or gaming marathon, unwind in our premium massage chairs. These state-of-the-art chairs offer various massage programs to help you relax and rejuvenate.

With multiple intensity levels and massage techniques, you can customize your experience to target specific areas of tension.

Perfect for solo relaxation or a quick break between activities, our massage chairs provide a spa-like experience right at our facility.
